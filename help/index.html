Controls

Default controls:

WASD to move
Shift to cancel velocity
R to respawn
U to zoom out
I to zoom in
O to toggle hitboxes
P to toggle WebSocket messages (client-only)
F to freecam (client-only)
Arrow keys to move camera (when in freecam mode)

You can change controls at the settings.



Powers

There are 12 powers (As of 12 Mar 2021)

Shrinker [0]

Shrinks your radius from 3 units to 2.25.
You can get this power by completing Rooms.

Explosion [1]

Kills all enemies (except immunes) within a 100-unit radius.
You can get this power by completing Minefield.

Build [2]

Places a 36 unit × 7 unit box underneath you.
Can be used to glitch into obstacles.
You can get this power by completing Prison (10).

Meteor [3]

Spawns a fireball in the direction of your cursor.
You can get this power by completing Battlefield.

Refuel [4]

Refills your fuel bar.
You can get this power by completing Turmoil.

Feather [5]

Halves your weight.
You can get this power by completing Space.

Shield [6]

Spawns a shield that can deflect enemies (again, except immunes).
You can get this power by completing Machinery.

Dash [7]

Moves you quickly in the direction of your cursor. When dashing, you are invinicble against enemies.
Can be used in the DoubleDash bugs.
You can get this power by completing Tunnel.

Lantern [8]

Leaves a path behind you that shrinks enemies.
You can get this power by completing Prison (14).

Ghost [9]

Rescues others. If used when dead, that slot is unusable. Has a long cooldown.
You can get this power by completing Nightmare.

Frost [10]

Slows enemies down.
You can get this power by completing Glacier.

Shell [11]

Acts as a meteor that follows you around.
You can get this power by completing Swamp.
